My Investment Approach

Investment Thesis

My investment thesis focuses on analyzing companies with strong fundamentals, and recognizing competitive advantages like high margins and monopolies. I also apply technical analysis to figure out stock bottoms or entry points for swing trades.

Investment Strategy

My strategy combines fundamental analysis and strict risk management. I utilize a mix of long-term stock or option holdings, and hedge with CC's or VIX if warranted. Capital allocation is structured to balance growth opportunities with downside protection, ensuring sustainable portfolio performance.

October 3, 2025

AI bubble?

Examining the factors driving recent AI stock performance, it is logical to assume that we might be in a bubble. I personally believe in many beneficiaries of the AI rally, many businesses will stand to benefit, especially NVDA which I've been holding since 2021. The fact remains, the giants are not slowing their spending on chips, and companies like micron, nvidia, and broadcom will keep seeing their shares skyrocket. As for the smaller cap & no revenue AI startups, they seem to be overblown and unreasonable. So are we in a bubble?
